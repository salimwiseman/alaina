SmartiAccounti Graphical Leaders Dashboard

SmartiAccounti Graphical Leaders Dashboard is a management information system (MIS) that allows executives and other stakeholders to define targets and monitor progress of key performance indicators (KPI) in order to monitor the contribution of the various departments in their organization. To gauge exactly how well an organization is performing overall, SmartiAccounti Graphical Leaders Dashboard allows you to capture and report specific data points from each department within the organization, thus providing a "snapshot" of performance.

SmartiAccounti Graphical Leaders Dashboard uses the day-to-day operational data stored in SmartiAccounti database. The stored data can be difficult to process in a raw format, so the SmartiAccounti Graphical Leaders Dashboard provides a user-friendly way of gaining insights, in a format that can be easily transferred between stakeholders, helping you making educated data driven decisions.

Benefits of using SmartiAccounti Graphical Leaders Dashboard

  • Graphical presentation of performance measures
  • Drill down functionality
  • Ability to identify and correct negative trends
  • Measure performance
  • Ability to generate detailed reports showing new trends
  • Ability to make more informed decisions based on collected business intelligence
  • Align strategies and organizational goals
  • Saves time compared to running multiple reports
  • Gain total visibility of all systems instantly
  • Quick identification of data outliers and correlations
