Point of Sale (POS) Module

SmartiAccounti fully supports Point of Sale (POS) technology that facilitates the retail sales transactions

What is POS?

SmartiAccounti comes with integrated POS module. But what is POS and what is POS module.

A Point of Sale (POS) is the place where and when retail sale transactions occur. POS module is a software that allow retail businesses to complete purchases, record sales data and view business performance reports, systematically.

SmartiAccounti POS module may use various devices such as weighing scales, barcode scanners, and cash registers. To make a payment, payment terminals, touch screens, and other hardware and software options are available.

This technology facilitates the retail sales transactions. SmartiAccounti POS module can also lower the cost of doing business while increasing productivity, resulting in improving your bottom line.

SmartiAccounti POS module will result in a fast return on investment (ROI), both in monetary value and time spent on day-to-day operations.

There is no better time than now to consider SmartiAccounti POS module for your business. SmartiAccounti POS module is easy to use and very cost-effective.

Benefits of SmartiAccounti POS Module

SmartiAccounti POS module comes with plenty of benefits for your business, especially when compared with the traditional, manual process of cash registers. With SmartiAccounti POS module, you’ll see benefits in every aspects of your business, not just at the checkout.

  1. Improved Efficiency:

    With the right systematic tools, cashier effectiveness will experience significant improvement. Armed with barcode scanner and SmartiAccounti POS module, the checkout time at the cash counter will drop down drastically, resulting in an increased efficiency.

  2. Ease of Use:

    SmartiAccounti POS module is very user-friendly and easy to learn. This would result in short time of training and in increased productivity, overall.

  3. Payment with Modern Methods:

    Given the advancement in the payment technology worldwide, the use of SmartiAccounti POS module can benefit the business by providing the customer with the convenience of paying by modern payment methods such as EMV chip payment cards, contactless payments (NFC), and mobile wallet payments such as Apple Pay, Android Pay, and Samsung Pay. Customer satisfaction and loyality will increase, resulting in increased revenue. Study shows that 44 percent of respondents prefer to pay using debit cards and 33 percent favor using credit cards as their preferred payment method.

  4. Higher Accuracy:

    The use of systematic SmartiAccounti POS module will eliminate the manual entry of items sold and their prices, thus minimizing the risk of human errors. It improves accuracy throughout the entire sale transaction, with real time data, automated calculations of transactions counts and amounts.

  5. Better Inventory Management:

    Unlike manual cash register, SmartiAccounti POS module includes advanced inventory management capabilities. From tracking a product’s quantity-on-hand to setting up re-order triggers and a retail matrix, SmartiAccounti POS module will save you hours of inventory management each week. And the best part is that it will also give you real-time inventory data, something that even the best cash registers cannot do.

  6. Fast and More Accurate Reporting:

    SmartiAccounti POS module comes with reporting features that allow you to keep a close eye on sales, profits, and expenses like Cost of Goods Sold (COGS). POS reports give you data in real-time, and formatted with easy-to-read information.

  7. Improved and Integrated Accounting:

    SmartiAccounti POS module helps in streamlining the accounting process. Old-fashioned cash registers force accountants to sort through hundreds of receipts, but with a SmartiAccounti POS module, you can print reports and import data directly from SmartiAccounti system.

  8. Customer Value-adding Receipts:

    SmartiAccounti POS module provide your customers with more detailed receipts rather than just a slip of paper with the date and the amount of the sale. SmartiAccounti POS module use inventory data to provide much more information, including item description, price, and savings from a sale or coupon. You also have the option to customize a particular portion of the receipt like the footer. You can use this to print coupons directly on the receipt, provide information on your loyalty rewards program, state your return policy, or promote your social media channels — or any other custom message.

  9. Faster Customer Service:

    Do you like to wait in long lines? We didn’t think so; we don’t either, and neither do your customers. In fact, if checkout lines are too long, customers will get frustrated and put back the items they intended to purchase and walk out. SmartiAccounti POS module provides a faster checkout process than cash registers, especially with the use of a barcode scanner to ring up the products. SmartiAccounti POS module allows you to make the most of the checkout experience to engage customers and make sure they leave with a good impression of your brand.
